Archipelago

A game made for CMPM 171 by Miles Marsh, Sean Rowley, Ewin Mathew, Mark Mcateer, and Samuel Liao

Archipelago is a first person game made in Unreal Engine 5, where you fall from island to island fighting enemies.
However, the only way you can move around the islands is by using your weapons to propel you in unique and intersting ways.
Can you claim the title of champion in the movement shooter without movement or shooting?

Prototype Demo Download Link (Windows) (3/5/2024)

To play our game, download the above link, unzip it, and click on the application named "Archipelago".
Controls:
WASD - Movement
Space - Jump
Mouse - Look around
Left Click - Basic Attack
Right Click - Dash Attack
Esc - Pause/Quit the game
