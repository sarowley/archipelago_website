Archipelago

A game made for CMPM 171 by Miles Marsh, Sean Rowley, Ewin Mathew, Mark Mcateer, and Samuel Liao

Archipelago is a first person rouge-lite game made in Unreal Engine 5, where you fall from island to island fighting enemies while collecting treasure.
However, the only way you can move around the islands is by using your weapon to propel you in unique and intersting ways.
This website is dedicated to the downloading and production of a vertical slice our team made for our game.

WASD/Arrow Keys - Movement

Mouse - Look around
Left Click (Tap) - Basic Attack
Left Click (Hold) - Walk Forward
Right Click - Dash Attack
Esc - Pause the game
